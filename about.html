Our Story

Samrat services Group was founded with a vision to connect people through both culture and innovation. We operate Samrat Kitchen, a restaurant offering traditional Nepali and Indian dishes and we aim to provide cutting-edge IT & Digital Services to local businesses nationwide and beyond. From our roots in Japan, we’ve grown into a brand that brings warmth to dining and precision to digital solutions. Our company initially started as a restaurant business and is now expanding into IT and digital services. The new division aims to provide digital transformation support for small hospitality businesses, including graphic design, website creation and document support services.

Our Values

  • Customer-centric approach
  • Quality-first mindset
  • Continuous improvement and innovation
  • Community connection

Mission

Our mission is to serve our community and clients with sincerity, flavor and innovation. Whether it's a delicious biryani or a complex software solution — we aim for excellence in everything we do.
