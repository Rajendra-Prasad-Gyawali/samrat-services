About Us

Who we are and what drives us

Our Story

Samrat services Group was founded with a vision to connect people through both culture and innovation. We operate Samrat Kitchen, a restaurant offering traditional Nepali and Indian dishes, and we also provide cutting-edge IT & Digital Services to businesses worldwide. From our roots in Japan, we’ve grown into a brand that brings warmth to dining and precision to digital solutions.

Mission

Our mission is to serve our community and clients with sincerity, flavor, and innovation. Whether it's a delicious biryani or a complex software solution — we aim for excellence in everything we do.

Our Values

  • Authenticity in food and services
  • Customer-centric approach
  • Quality-first mindset
  • Continuous improvement and innovation
  • Community connection
